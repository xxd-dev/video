edit subbox

here you find a list of all your subscriptions. you can delete or add channels and edit rules for them.
once your're done, don't forget to scroll to the end and press save!
min is the duration a video has to exceed, formatted like "0:45" or "1:00:00"
min is the duration a video must not exceed, formatted like "15:00" or "1:30:00"
need is a list of words that need to be contained in the title, formatted like "gameplay,video" or "song"
avoid is a list of words that can not be contained in the title, formatted like "shorts,podcast" or "prank"
